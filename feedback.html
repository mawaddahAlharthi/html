Movie Reviews

Fast & Furious

Rating: ★★★★☆

"Fast & Furious" is a high-octane action-packed movie franchise that keeps fans on the edge of their seats. The film's thrilling car chases, heists, and explosive stunts make it one of the most popular action films worldwide.

The Hangover

Rating: ★★★☆☆

"The Hangover" is a comedic masterpiece that follows the wild misadventures of a group of friends after a bachelor party in Las Vegas. The film delivers non-stop laughs with its outrageous scenarios and hilarious performances.

Welad Rizk (ولاد رزق)

Rating: ★★★★☆

"Welad Rizk" is an intense Egyptian crime thriller that revolves around a group of brothers involved in illegal activities. Their loyalty is tested when a heist goes wrong.

Joker

Rating: ★★☆☆☆

"Joker," starring Joaquin Phoenix, is a dark, psychological portrayal of one man’s descent into madness. Phoenix’s Oscar-winning performance is haunting and powerful, making this one of the most thought-provoking films of recent times.

Artist Reviews

Rabeh Saqer (رابح صقر)

Rating: ★★★★★

Rabeh Saqer is a legendary Saudi singer known for his emotional and heartfelt songs. With a career spanning over two decades, his unique voice and style have earned him a massive following across the Arab world. His concerts are known for their energy and connection with fans.

Abdul Majeed Abdullah (عبد المجيد عبدالله)

Rating: ★★★★★

Abdul Majeed Abdullah is one of the most prominent figures in Arabic music. Known for his poetic lyrics and melodious voice, he has captured the hearts of millions. His songs often reflect themes of love and longing, making him a beloved artist in the Arab music scene.
